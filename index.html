Welcome

Welcome to CUTS, your home away from home. Whether you're a Thai student, have spent time in Thailand, or are simply interested in Thai culture, we're thrilled to have you here!

Moving to a new city or country can feel overwhelming. I arrived in Cambridge after a gap year, and like many new students, I was struck by how intense and fast-paced everything felt. Amid the Cambridge workload, I found comfort and community in CUTS. It became my place to unwind, a space that felt like home.

Throughout the year, CUTS hosts a wide variety of activities across the three terms. From snacks and board games to karaoke nights and badminton sessions, there's something for everyone. Our major annual events include Thai Night in Lent Term and the Oxbridge Games in Michaelmas, when we welcome our Thai friends from Oxf*rd. We also love seeing members take initiative, so if there's an event you'd like to see, just let the committee know!

Beyond social events, CUTS also supports your future. We regularly host career-focused sessions, including talks from organisations like the Thailand Development Research Institute (TDRI), and we'll keep you updated on career and networking opportunities, especially those happening in London.

As your President this year, I'm committed to making CUTS as welcoming and inclusive as possible. Our goal is to ensure our events not only bring joy, but also help you thrive during your time at Cambridge.

I look forward to meeting you soon!
